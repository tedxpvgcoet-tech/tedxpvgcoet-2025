2021 - Avant Garde

We hope to encapsulate the notion of people who seem ahead of their time, and introducing their own forms of art, may they seem unconventional. The TEDx theme "Avant Garde" encapsulates a spirit of innovation, pushing boundaries beyond conventional norms. Rooted in the French term for "advance guard," it embraces cutting-edge ideas, unconventional perspectives, and visionary thinking. Avant Garde challenges the status quo, encouraging speakers and attendees to explore uncharted intellectual territories. This theme fosters an environment where forward-thinking concepts and groundbreaking insights emerge, inspiring a collective journey into the forefront of ideas. It celebrates the pioneers and provocateurs, urging us to break free from the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary in our pursuit of knowledge and progress.

Taufiq Qureshi

Taufiq Qureshi, an ace percussionist of India, referred to as a game changer, is also an acclaimed composer. Taufiq is recognized for his path-breaking and trend-setting work, as a performing artiste and as composer/arranger in the field of world music.

Ayesha Billimoria

Ayesha Billimoria is a woman of many talents and possesses an innumerable collection of titles: she was named “fastest girl of India” in her teenage years, represented Maharashtra for 17 years as an athlete, holds a three-time national championship in the 200m sprint and has also partaken in rigorous training to prepare for the Olympics

kuntal Joisher

For Kuntal Joisher, his whole life played out before him when he, along with other mountaineers, was trapped at Camp 2 of Mt. Everest during the devastating avalanche triggered by a massive Earthquake in April, 2015. The near death experience triggered in him a storm of emotions which would change his entire life

Priyanka Kasture

Embarking in 2018, Priyanka has created and leveraged powerful content marketing strategies to build a cumulative follower-base of 500,000 people on leading social media platforms, and built a thriving multi-figure business by monetising her influence.

Shreenand Bapat

Dr. Shreenand Bapat is a scholar with a PhD in Sanskrit and an MA in AIHC & Archaeology. He is a curator at the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute, Managing Editor at Annals of the BORI, and guest lecturer at Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth and Chanakya Mandal Pariwar.
